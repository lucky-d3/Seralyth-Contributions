# 💞 Contribution Policy
Find a bug in the menu? Create an [issue](https://github.com/Seralyth/Seralyth-Menu/issues)! We'd be glad to fix it. Make sure to use the issue formatting to as much as your ability as possible, fill in blanks, and keep titles short.
Wish to contribute to the menu, or maybe even fix an issue yourself? Make a [pull request](https://github.com/Seralyth/Seralyth-Menu/issues). We currently do not accept any new code into the project that was written largely, entirely, or even mostly by an LLM. All contributions should be made by humans that understand the codebase.
**As long as it works** and it isn't **non-assistantly made / generated by AI**, you should be fine!
Provide your Discord username, and I'll even give you a special role in the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/seralyth)!
